What We Do

Core Capabilities

Six integrated capability areas — each grounded in operational experience, each deployable as a standalone service or as part of a unified awareness architecture.

Airborne Reconnaissance & ISR

Persistent overhead coverage using tactical UAS platforms with EO/IR sensor integration, AI-assisted target recognition, and GPS-denied operation. Built for the environments where standard platforms fail.

Situational Awareness Systems

Real-time common operating pictures using ATAK-compatible C2 infrastructure, Doodle Labs mesh radio, and multi-sensor fusion. Decision-grade information at the speed of operations.

Integrated Perimeter Security

Combined aerial and ground-based surveillance unifying UAS, compact radar for C-UAS detection, EO/IR payloads, and MANET communications into a single persistent common operating picture.

CI/HUMINT-Informed Doctrine

Operational planning and threat vulnerability analysis grounded in counterintelligence and human intelligence disciplines. Solutions are mission-shaped, not product-shaped.

Threat & Vulnerability Assessment

Site surveys, gap analysis, and security architecture review for facilities, perimeters, and critical infrastructure. Delivered by operators who have worked the problem from the inside.

Operator Training & Integration

UAS operator qualification, platform-specific TTPs, and integration training delivered through the Vets to Drones workforce pipeline — operators trained the same way we operate.
